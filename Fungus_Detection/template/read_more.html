Most Common Fungus In Our Environment

Aspergillus

Location: Found in soil, decaying organic matter, dust, and air conditioning systems. It is common in damp indoor environments.

Cladosporium

Location: Present in both indoor and outdoor environments, commonly found on plants, in soil, and household dust.

Stachybotrys

Location: Found in buildings with water damage, on wallpaper, ceiling tiles, and insulation materials.

Penicillium

Location: Common in soil, decaying vegetation, indoor air, and household dust.

Alternaria

Location: Common in soil, air, and plant debris. Found indoors in damp areas like bathrooms and basements.

Rhizopus

Location: Found in soil, decaying fruits, vegetables, and bread. Also found in compost.

Candida

Location: Found on human skin, in the mouth, intestines, and hospitals.

Fusarium

Location: Found in soil, plants, and water. Often contaminates grains.

Cryptococcus

Location: Found in soil, pigeon droppings, and decaying wood.
